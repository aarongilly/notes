**Projects are Time-bound and completable. Areas are not.**

## Source
- [[PARA Method]]

# Differences

## Projects

1. Have a deadline
2. Are completable
3. Associated with goals to be achieved
4. Measured by OKRs

## Areas

1. Have no deadline
2. Cannot be "finished"
3. Associated with standards to be upheld
4. Measured by KPIs
