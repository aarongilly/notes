# Basic Description

Barbell put upon the shoulders, steadied by the hands. The body is lowered by the legs until the thighs are parallel with the ground, then raised back to standing.

## Muscles Worked

- Quads
- Hamstrings
- Glutes
- Abs (indirect)
- Spinal erectors
